# JelloChat Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 13, 2026

**Experimental Service.** JelloChat is an experimental service. Features, availability, and data practices may change without notice.

## 1. Overview
JelloChat ("we", "our", "us") provides chat, community, and voice features. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your choices.

## 2. Information We Collect
We may collect and store:

- Account data: username, email address, password hash.
- Verification and security data: email verification tokens, password reset tokens, login/session tokens.
- User content: server messages, direct messages, and moderation-related records.
- Social data: friend requests, friendships, server membership.
- Community data: servers, channels, invites, bans, moderation actions.
- Technical data: basic logs needed to operate and secure the service.

We do not store plaintext passwords.

## 3. How We Use Information
We use your information to:

- Create and manage your account.
- Authenticate users and keep accounts secure.
- Provide chat, DM, server, friend, invite, and moderation features.
- Send service emails (for example verification and password reset).
- Detect abuse, enforce community rules, and maintain platform safety.
- Improve reliability and performance.

## 4. Voice and Real-Time Features
Voice features may rely on LiveKit infrastructure (self-hosted or managed). Real-time signaling and media routing are required to provide voice connectivity.

## 5. Sharing of Information
We do not sell your personal data. We may share data with service providers strictly to operate the service (for example SMTP email provider, hosting, and voice infrastructure).

## 6. Data Retention
We retain account and service data as long as needed to operate the service, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements.

When you delete your account, related data is removed according to system behavior and database constraints.

## 7. Security
We use reasonable technical and organizational safeguards. No system is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

## 8. Your Choices and Rights
You can:

- Update account details.
- Request password reset.
- Delete your account.

Depending on your location, you may have additional privacy rights under applicable law.

## 9. Children
JelloChat is not intended for children under 13 (or higher minimum age required by your local law).

## 10. International Users
Your information may be processed in countries other than your own, subject to applicable law.

## 11. Changes to This Policy
We may update this policy. We will update the "Last updated" date when changes are made.

## 12. Contact
For privacy questions, contact: privacy@jellodog.com
